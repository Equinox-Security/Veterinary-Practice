Our Services

Dover Veterinary Hospital

Wellness Exams

Comprehensive annual and semi-annual physical examinations to keep your pet healthy at every life stage.

Vaccinations

Core and lifestyle vaccines tailored to your pet's age, health status, and risk factors.

Parasite Control

Prevention and treatment for fleas, ticks, heartworm, and intestinal parasites.

Nutritional Counseling

Personalized dietary recommendations to support your pet's health and longevity.

Behavioral Counseling

Expert guidance on behavior challenges, anxiety, and training support.

Microchip ID

Permanent identification to help reunite you with a lost pet.

Radiology / X-Ray

Digital radiography for fast, accurate diagnosis of bone, joint, and internal conditions.

In-House Laboratory

Same-day blood panels, urinalysis, and diagnostic testing for rapid results.

Ultrasonography

Non-invasive imaging for detailed evaluation of internal organs.

Surgical Services

Safe, skilled surgical procedures in a fully equipped facility with attentive post-operative care.

Anesthesia & Pain Management

Modern anesthesia protocols and multimodal pain management for your pet's comfort and safety.

Dental Care

Professional dental cleaning, extractions, and at-home oral health guidance.

Oncology

Compassionate evaluation and management of cancer in companion animals.

Holistic Medicine

Integrative approaches that complement conventional veterinary care.

Acupuncture

Traditional acupuncture therapy for pain management, neurological conditions, and overall wellness.

End of Life Care

Compassionate support for you and your pet through life's most difficult transition.

In-House Pharmacy

Convenient on-site dispensing of medications and preventatives.

Prescription Refills

Easy refill requests for ongoing medications — call ahead or ask at your visit.

New Client Registration

Welcome new patients — registration forms and information available online or at your first visit.
