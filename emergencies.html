Signs of a Pet Emergency

Seek immediate veterinary care if your pet shows any of the following:

  • Difficulty breathing, choking, or persistent coughing
  • Collapse, inability to stand, or sudden weakness
  • Seizures or sudden loss of coordination
  • Suspected poisoning or ingestion of a toxic substance
  • Severe bleeding that cannot be controlled
  • Trauma from a car accident, fall, or animal attack
  • Eye injuries or sudden vision changes
  • Inability to urinate, especially in male cats
  • Distended or painful abdomen
  • Loss of consciousness or unresponsiveness
